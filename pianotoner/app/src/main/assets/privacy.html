PianoToner implements a sine-wave generator, which is touch-driven by a virtual, and scrollable, piano-like keyboard displayed on the screen.
The note frequency of the middle “A” can be manually adjusted to reflect the following four Concert Pitch frequencies: the standard 440Hz, the baroque 415Hz, the uncommon 435Hz from the 1859 French “diapason normal” standard and the highest 445Hz tuning.
